About Us

The world is our vineyard. WineShop At Home, founded in 2005, is headquartered in the legendary Napa Valley. Our winery, owned by the Fredrick family of Dallas, Texas, produces award-winning, exclusive, artisan wines across 50 brands. Our WineShop At Home Artisan Wines™ family of brands offer a unique taste at every price point. Explore our extensive array of wine varietals and exclusive blends. From sweet and bubbly to dry and crisp whites, smooth and supple to bold and intense reds, we feature artisan wines for every occasion, palate and personality.
